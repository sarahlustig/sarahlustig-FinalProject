Sarah Lustig's Final Project!

My name is Sarah Lustig and this is where you can find my final project for CSC106.
I worked on this project with Kate Ciulla. We created an air hockey game.
On this page you can view our game and get a better understanding of our program.
To use this program simply read the instructions and click the play button.
You will then be brought to the game screen. Here simply move your mouse to control the red paddle.
Hit the puck and try to score on your oppenent's goal. You'll have to score 5 points in order to win!
The purpose of our game is for enjoyment and entertainment so have fun!
